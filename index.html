Our Values

Diversity

Our shared background is in the retail/wholesale Bicycle/Outdoor industry where like the IT industry diversity is lacking. Projects/initiatives/companies are more successful when diverse talent is present and engaged. We believe that a partnership model is more profitable, diversity promotes better ideas and solutions, and practicing good corporate responsibility is an expectation in the 21st Century.
To that end Origma exists to diversify the IT and Outdoor industries. We believe that a path to success in North America's service economy can be drawn through the relatively blue collar Outdoor industry or the relatively white collar IT industry. Origma has committed 15% of staff time and 15% of profits to Open Source Software projects and organizations/initiatives who introduce computer science to students from underserved communities.

Our Process

We work closely with our partners to define, map, and prioritize the needs of all stakeholders. From there we prioritize project features and develop prototype that is incrementally built upon with partner feedback. The result is a collaborative process that works well for any business regardless of project timeline or budget.
Why Origma? Origma is the genus (family name) of the Rockwarbler - a small bird found only wooded/rocky areas in New South Wales. The inspiration came from our experience in the bicycle industry where new product is often named after/inspired by the natural world. What we do With a background in business operations and a focus on sustainable business practices, we employ agile practices to quickly deliver results. We employ web technologies to deliver long term value to our partners in the Retail/Wholesale and Outdoor industries.
