Our Process

We work closely with our partners to define, map, and prioritize the needs of all stakeholders. From there we prioritize project features and develop prototype that is incrementally built upon with partner feedback. The result is a collaborative process that works well for any business regardless of project timeline or budget.
Why Origma? Origma is the genus (family name) of the rockwarbler - a small bird found only wooded/rocky areas in New South Wales. The inspiration came from our experience in the bicycle industry where product naming is often inspired by nature. What we do With a background in business operations and a focus on sustainable business practices, we employ agile methodologies to identify and balance the needs of all stakeholders. We employ web technologies to deliver long term value to our partners in the Retail/Wholesale and Outdoor industries.
